3. Aerospace & Physics Engine
The core "brains" of the app. These packages handle the heavy mathematical equations defined in the PRD.
* Satellite.js (v5.0.0): * Role: The primary implementation of the SGP4 Propagation algorithm.
   * Function: Converts TLE (Two-Line Element) data into ECI (Earth-Centered Inertial) coordinates.
* CesiumJS (v1.120):
   * Role: High-fidelity 3D geospatial mapping.
   * Function: Renders the 4K oblate spheroid Earth and manages the 3D debris tilesets.
* Lambert-Solver (v2.1.0):
   * Role: Solves the Lambert Intercept Problem.
   * Function: Calculates velocity vectors (Δv) for the transfer arcs.
* Math.js (v13.0.0):
   * Role: High-precision arithmetic for the Tsiolkovsky and NRV equations.
   * Function: Prevents floating-point errors in deep-space distance calculations.
